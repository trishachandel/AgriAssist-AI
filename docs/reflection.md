# Reflection Note

## Project Overview

This project uses Google's Gemini AI model to provide agricultural advisory support through a chatbot interface.

## Limitations

- AI-generated responses may not always be accurate.
- Crop diseases often require visual inspection for proper diagnosis.
- Recommendations may vary depending on local climate and soil conditions.
- Internet connectivity is required for AI responses.

## Responsible AI Considerations

- The chatbot is restricted to agriculture-related topics.
- A disclaimer is displayed to users.
- Users are advised to verify recommendations with licensed agricultural extension officers.
- The chatbot is intended to assist farmers and supervisors, not replace professional experts.

## Future Improvements

- Image-based disease detection.
- Weather-based recommendations.
- Multi-language support (Hindi and English).
- Location-specific crop guidance.
